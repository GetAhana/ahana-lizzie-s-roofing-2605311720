Who we are

Built on trust

Lizz Roof is a Toronto-based roofing company with 15+ years of experience and a family heritage rooted in excellence. We specialize in residential roof replacement, commercial flat roofing, and new construction projects across the Greater Toronto Area. With a 4.8-star rating from 25 verified customers, we're known for our integrity, professionalism, and respect for your home.

Our story

Why we do us

Roofing runs deep in our family. For over 30 years, the roofing trade has been our passion and expertise. We started Lizz Roof to bring that same dedication, skill, and integrity to every residential and commercial project in Toronto and beyond. We believe a quality roof is the foundation of a safe, protected home—and we take that responsibility seriously.

Our owner-operated approach means you're not just working with a company; you're working with someone who genuinely cares about your project. Every roof we replace, every flat roofing installation, and every new construction job reflects our commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

We've built our reputation on showing up on time, respecting your property, and delivering results that last. Whether it's a residential roof replacement or a large commercial installation, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.

Roofing is in our blood. We do this work because we love it, and we show that love in every project.
